Do Immediately

Apply for Sri Lanka ETA Visa
Apply at eta.gov.lk — ~$35–50, usually approved within 24hrs.
Do now
✓
Book hill country train tickets
Colombo–Galle coastal = no booking needed, buy unreserved on the day. The Colombo→Kandy and Anuradhapura→Kandy (via Polgahawela) legs have reserved cars that sell out — book via 12go.asia or Bookaway.com. ⚠️ Verify hill country route is running — cyclone damage in 2025.
Do now
✓
Book accommodation
Booking.com, 3-star, score 8.0+. 8 bases: Colombo (Sep 20–21), Anuradhapura (Sep 21–23), Kandy (Sep 23–25), Colombo (Sep 25–26), Galle (Sep 26–28), Tangalle (Sep 28–30), Colombo (Sep 30–Oct 2).
Do now
✓
Notify your bank
Tell your bank you're in Sri Lanka Sep 20 – Oct 2, otherwise cards may be blocked on first use.

Money Tips
💱
Exchange rate
~300 LKR = $1. Check nearer the time.
🏧
ATMs
Withdraw before Tangalle — fewer ATMs there. Anuradhapura also has limited ATMs so withdraw in Colombo before heading north.
💵
Cash is king
Local restaurants, tuk-tuks, smaller hotels often won't take cards.
🍛
Food costs
Rice and curry: $2–4. Kottu roti: ~$2. Tourist restaurant: $6–12. Budget $15–20/day.
Key Info
🛂
ETA Visa
Apply at eta.gov.lk — ~$35–50.
🚆
Train Bookings
Hill country reserved: 12go.asia or Bookaway.com. Colombo–Galle coastal: unreserved, buy at the station on the day. ⚠️ Check hill country route status — cyclone damage in 2025.
📱
Apps
PickMe — metered tuk-tuks, no haggling. Dialog SIM at arrivals (~$5–10).
💊
Health
Hep A + Typhoid recommended. Malaria tablets for Anuradhapura. DEET repellent. Bottled water only.
🌧️
Weather
Tail end of south-west monsoon. Short heavy rain, usually clears. Cultural Triangle drier than south coast.
🔌
Plugs + Time
Type G (UK) plugs. UTC+5:30. Late Sep: 4.5 hrs ahead of UK (BST).
🛕
Temple Etiquette
Cover shoulders + knees. Shoes off. No selfies with Buddha statues.
🛺
Tuk-tuks
Use PickMe where possible. If flagging one down, always agree price before getting in.
