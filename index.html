Money Tips
💱
Exchange rate
~300 LKR = $1. Check nearer the time.
🏧
ATMs
Withdraw before Tangalle — fewer ATMs there. Also withdraw in Colombo before heading north to Anuradhapura.
💵
Cash is king
Local restaurants, tuk-tuks, smaller hotels often won't take cards.
🍛
Food costs
Rice and curry: $2–4. Kottu roti: ~$2. Tourist restaurant: $6–12. Budget $15–20/day.
Key Info
🛂
ETA Visa
Apply at eta.gov.lk — ~$35–50.
🚆
Train Bookings
Hill country reserved: 12go.asia or Bookaway.com. Colombo–Galle coastal: unreserved, buy at the station. ⚠️ Check hill country route status — cyclone damage in 2025.
📱
Apps
PickMe — metered tuk-tuks, no haggling. Dialog SIM at arrivals (~$5–10).
💊
Health
Hep A + Typhoid recommended. Malaria tablets for Anuradhapura. DEET repellent. Bottled water only.
🌧️
Weather
Tail end of south-west monsoon. Short heavy rain. Cultural Triangle drier than south coast.
🔌
Plugs + Time
Type G (UK) plugs. UTC+5:30. Late Sep: 4.5 hrs ahead of UK (BST).
🛕
Temple Etiquette
Cover shoulders + knees. Shoes off. No selfies with Buddha statues.
🛺
Tuk-tuks
Use PickMe where possible. If flagging one down, always agree price before getting in.
